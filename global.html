Global AI Trends

Generative & Multimodal AI Take Center Stage

Generative models (e.g. GPT, DALL‑E, Llama, DeepSeek) are now core tools for content creation across text, visuals, video, and even code.
Multimodal AI—capable of understanding and generating across formats like text, audio, images, and video—is increasingly powerful and integrated into industry workflows

Rise of Agentic AI — Autonomous Smart Agents

AI agents (aka "agentic AI") now perform multi-step tasks, manage tools, and act autonomously—far beyond chatbots. They’re used in domains like sales, marketing, customer support, and scheduling.

More Info

Hyper‑Personalization & Decision Intelligence

Businesses leverage AI to tailor experiences in real time (fitness, retail, marketing) and optimize decisions (staff scheduling, pricing, logistics).

More Info

Edge AI & Energy‑Efficient Infrastructure

AI is shifting from centralized cloud to edge devices—smartphones, wearables, IoT appliances—for faster, private inference. In fact, 30–60% of devices now include local AI capabilities.

More Info

AI in Healthcare, Science & R&D

AI-led breakthroughs in drug discovery, diagnostics, and preventive care are accelerating—e.g. 30%+ of new drugs expected from generative AI in 2025. AI-assisted imaging and predictive tools improve early detection of cancer and Alzheimer’s.
